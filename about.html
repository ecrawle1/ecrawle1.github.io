Overview

I’m a Data Specialist at Kent State University’s Office of Scholarships & Financial Aid, where I build SQL-driven solutions, streamline processes in Ellucian Banner 9 and NextGen Dynamic Forms, and collaborate with IT and functional teams to improve data quality, compliance, and the student experience.

My path moved from administrative support to data and application development, and I thrive at the intersection of problem-solving, communication, and secure, efficient systems.

Professional Philosophy

Technology and data should empower people. I focus on translating complex requirements into clear, reliable solutions—pairing curiosity and documentation with an emphasis on accuracy, accessibility, and outcomes.

Key Strengths

  • Translating business needs into secure, scalable SQL and application workflows.
  • Designing and optimizing Oracle SQL and Cognos reporting for compliance and decision support.
  • Building automations and data validations in Banner 9 and NextGen Dynamic Forms.
  • Creating clear SOPs and training to help teams adopt new tools with confidence.
  • Collaborating across IT, finance, and student services to deliver practical results.

Beyond Work

I mentor students in FIRST LEGO League and FIRST Tech Challenge—where creativity, coding, and teamwork come together. Lifelong interests in science, nature, and storytelling shape how I approach challenges: with evidence, imagination, and exploration.

What’s Next

I’m pursuing an M.S. in Business Analytics at Kent State, strengthening my work in predictive modeling, visualization, and decision science to support enterprise applications and data-driven decisions.
