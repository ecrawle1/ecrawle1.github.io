Automated Batch Posting, AppXtender Integration & GLRSLCT Selections

Built Banner 9 population and variable definitions as well as writing out steps used in automations for nightly batch posting and downstream processes, integrated with AppXtender, and used the defined selections for automatic document merging and selecting targeted communications.

Stack
Oracle SQL, Ellucian Banner 9, AppXtender, IBM Cognos
Role
Design & implementation of batch jobs, data flows, and letter generation; SOPs & handoffs
Outcome
Faster postings, fewer manual steps, traceable document indexing, and consistent letter output.
  • Designed and tuned Banner 9 population selection used by scheduled jobs for **automated batch posting** and import/export processes.
  • Integrated with **Xtender** for output indexing/attachments tied to import/export and transaction records.
  • Leveraged **GLRSLCT** population selections for letter creation and targeted outreach.
