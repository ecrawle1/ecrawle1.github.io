As a graduate of the Occupational Workplace Environment (OWE) program offered through the Four Cities Compact (Barberton, Norton, Copley, and Wadsworth schools), I graduated in 2000 as a member of the National Technical and Vocational Honor Society. The program enforced a strong sense of discipline and determination for its students, and I am proud to be one of its graduates.

As a college student, I pursued a Middle Childhood Education degree with a focus in Science and Language Arts. My research and teaching interests include astronomy and fictional literature. I hope to take students on an adventure through uncharted territory using imagination and a discovery of the truth and evidence that surrounds us.
